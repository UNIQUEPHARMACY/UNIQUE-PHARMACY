Our Story

Unique Pharmacy was founded in December 2024 by Mr. Faisal Hamdulay, with the strong support of Branch Head Mr. Saeed Bukhari, with a vision to deliver trusted healthcare with exceptional value.

From day one, we entered the market with highly competitive offers and customer-focused service, quickly earning the trust and appreciation of the Bhendi Bazar community. Our commitment to affordability, authenticity, and care has been at the core of our growth.

This outlet marks the third flagship pharmacy under Mr. Faisal Hamdulay’s leadership, following the success of stores in Mazgaon and Khed, Maharashtra. Each location reflects the same dedication to quality service and community trust.

At Unique Pharmacy, we aim to make healthcare more accessible, reliable, and convenient — building lasting relationships while serving every customer with care and integrity.
